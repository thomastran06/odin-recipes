Fried Rice Recipe

What is Fried Rice?

Fried rice is a popular dish of stir-fried cooked rice, typically mixed with eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat, and seasoned with soy sauce and aromatics like garlic.

Ingredients:

  • Cold, Day-Old Rice
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Garlic, Ginger, Onions

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Rice: Use cold, day-old rice to ensure a firm, non-clumpy texture. If using fresh, spread it on a sheet pan to cool and dry completely.
  2. Prep Ingredients: Finely chop aromatics and vegetables. Beat eggs with a pinch of salt
  3. Head the Pan: Head a wok or large skillet over high head until smojing, then add oil.
  4. Cook Ingredients Separately:
    • Eggs: Scramble eggs quickly, then remove from the pan and set aside.
    • Vegatables/aromatics: Stir-fry vegatables and aromatics until tender.
  5. Combine and Fry:
    • Add the cold rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps
    • Stir-fry on height heat for 1-2 minutes until the rice is hot and slightly toasted
    • Add soy sauce, sesame oil, and any other sauces
    • Return the cooked eggs to the pan, mixing everything together
  6. Finish and Serve: Garnish with green onions and serve.
